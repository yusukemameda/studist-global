Solution Planner (Tokyo)

Sales Tokyo, Japan Full-time

Overview

As a Solution Planner, you will be the primary driver of Teachme Biz adoption among Japanese enterprises with operations in ASEAN. You will own the full sales cycle — from discovery and demo to proposal, negotiation, and close — while collaborating closely with our teams in Bangkok and Ho Chi Minh City to ensure a seamless customer experience.

Responsibilities

  • Identify, research, and prospect target accounts — mid-to-large Japanese companies with manufacturing, logistics, or retail operations in ASEAN
  • Conduct tailored product demos that connect Teachme Biz capabilities to each customer's operational challenges
  • Build compelling proposals and ROI cases; negotiate and close contracts
  • Manage pipeline and forecast accurately via CRM
  • Partner with Customer Success for smooth onboarding and long-term account health
  • Share customer insights with the product team to inform the roadmap

Requirements

  • Native or business-level Japanese (required)
  • Business-level English (required)
  • 2+ years of B2B sales experience; SaaS or HR tech preferred
  • Strong consultative selling and presentation skills
  • Self-starter comfortable managing multiple deals simultaneously

Nice to Have

  • Background in manufacturing, logistics, retail, or food service industries
  • Experience selling to operations, HR, or training stakeholders
  • Basic Thai or Vietnamese language skills

What We Offer

  • Competitive base salary + performance bonus
  • Hybrid work — 3 days office / 2 days remote
  • International team across Japan, Thailand, and Vietnam
  • Company-sponsored visits to our ASEAN offices
  • Fast-track career growth in a scaling global company
  • Full social insurance, commuter allowance, 20+ days annual leave
