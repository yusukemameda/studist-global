Solution Planner Professional (Tokyo)

Sales Tokyo, Japan Full-time

Overview

The Solution Planner Professional role is designed for seasoned sales professionals who want to drive strategic, high-value deals. You will lead enterprise-level engagements with major Japanese corporations operating across ASEAN, acting as a trusted advisor on operational transformation. This role requires deep consultative skills, the ability to navigate complex organizations, and a passion for building long-term partnerships.

Responsibilities

  • Own a portfolio of strategic enterprise accounts and new business targets with significant ASEAN operations
  • Lead executive-level discovery sessions to understand operational challenges at depth
  • Design and deliver comprehensive solution proposals involving multi-site, multi-language Teachme Biz deployments
  • Drive complex, multi-stakeholder deal cycles from initial engagement through legal, procurement, and IT approval
  • Develop and execute account expansion strategies post-close
  • Mentor junior Solution Planners and contribute to team best practices
  • Represent Studist Global at industry events and customer forums

Requirements

  • Native or business-level Japanese (required)
  • Business-level English (required)
  • 5+ years of enterprise B2B sales experience; SaaS, ERP, or operations technology preferred
  • Proven track record of consistently achieving or exceeding quota
  • Strong executive presence with experience engaging C-suite and VP-level buyers
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills in both Japanese and English

Nice to Have

  • Deep knowledge of manufacturing, logistics, supply chain, or retail industries
  • Prior experience selling into Japanese companies with ASEAN operations
  • Familiarity with Salesforce or similar CRM platforms
  • Thai or Vietnamese language ability

What We Offer

  • Senior-level compensation package: competitive base + uncapped performance bonus
  • Hybrid work — flexible arrangement for experienced hires
  • Direct access to executive leadership and strategic decision-making
  • International team across Japan, Thailand, and Vietnam
  • Company-sponsored visits to our ASEAN offices
  • Full social insurance, commuter allowance, 20+ days annual leave

Interested? Send us your resume and a short note about yourself.
