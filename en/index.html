Mission & Vision

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission

Transforming the Future of Work Through Operations

Japan achieved remarkable economic growth during the Showa era, often referred to as the "Asian Miracle," driven by its population bonus.
The corporate rules, business processes, procedures, and systems developed during that time functioned effectively within the social environment of the era.
However, as the population bonus era has come to an end and labor shortages intensify, we must now update the systems of society that were built on the assumption of continuous population growth.
At Studist, we focus on operations—the operating system of organizational management.
By promoting visualization, standardization, simplification, and systemization (thorough implementation) of business activities, we work to modernize operations.
Our mission is to transform the way people work, enabling both companies and their employees to grow together, and to help create a society where innovation continues to thrive.

Vision

The future we aim for

To build a society that is filled with knowledge power driven by the true understanding and creative thinking.

If communication became simpler, both the sender and receiver of the message will no longer have to waste time on unnecessary things but can spend more time on creative things instead.
This will allow the society to be built with knowledge power-driven by the true understanding and creative thinking. And that is truly Studist's vision.

Teachme Biz

A business manual creation and sharing platform used in manufacturing, logistics, retail, food service, and other front-line operations. With multilingual support and smartphone-based use, it enables consistent procedures and quality at overseas locations.

  • Easily create SOPs anyone can understand with video and image-based content
  • Multilingual support (Japanese, English, Thai, Vietnamese, etc.) for multicultural workplaces
  • Access anytime from smartphones, streamlining on-site training
  • Visualize training status with viewing logs, comprehension tests, and reports
  • Local language support in Thailand & Vietnam with on-site implementation assistance
