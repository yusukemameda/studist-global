02 Mission & Vision

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission

Transforming the Future of Work Through Operations

Japan achieved remarkable economic growth during the Showa era, often referred to as the "Asian Miracle," driven by its population bonus.
The corporate rules, business processes, procedures, and systems developed during that time functioned effectively within the social environment of the era.
However, as the population bonus era has come to an end and labor shortages intensify, we must now update the systems of society that were built on the assumption of continuous population growth.
At Studist, we focus on operations—the operating system of organizational management.
By promoting visualization, standardization, simplification, and systemization (thorough implementation) of business activities, we work to modernize operations.
Our mission is to transform the way people work, enabling both companies and their employees to grow together, and to help create a society where innovation continues to thrive.
Vision

The future we aim for

To build a society that is filled with knowledge power driven by the true understanding and creative thinking.

If communication became simpler, both the sender and receiver of the message will no longer have to waste time on unnecessary things but can spend more time on creative things instead.
This will allow the society to be built with knowledge power-driven by the true understanding and creative thinking. And that is truly Studist's vision.

04 Product

Teachme Series

Creating engines of growth capital for the future, for every organization on the front lines

Teachme Biz

A business manual creation and sharing platform used in manufacturing, logistics, retail, food service, and other front-line operations. With multilingual support and smartphone-based use, it enables consistent procedures and quality at overseas locations.

  • Easily create SOPs anyone can understand with video and image-based content
  • Multilingual support (Japanese, English, Thai, Vietnamese, etc.) for multicultural workplaces
  • Access anytime from smartphones, streamlining on-site training
  • Visualize training status with viewing logs, comprehension tests, and reports
  • Local language support in Thailand & Vietnam with on-site implementation assistance
Japan Site Global Site

Teachme Process

A product that turns complex "business processes" into an easy-to-understand "map" through conversational AI. It visualizes the big picture that sits above individual manuals (procedures), and with three values—draw it, connect it, improve it—supports the standardization and improvement of work that has become person-dependent.

  • Draw the full picture of any business process just by feeding documents or chatting with AI
  • Switch between flowchart, list, and swimlane views with a single click
  • Link each step to Teachme Biz manuals, standard times, departments, and workloads
  • AI suggests what to automate, what to outsource, and what only people should do (Routing)
  • Auto-generate an efficiency-improvement report with direction, priorities, and concrete actions

Teachme Communication

A product that turns everyday communication into an asset that helps the front line, beyond language barriers. AI real-time translation removes the language wall so that knowledge sharing and daily information flow never stall—even in multinational, multi-generational workplaces.

  • Connect multilingual workplaces seamlessly with AI real-time translation chat
  • Daily reports that AI auto-formats from what you say (with translation and summary)
  • Resolve person-dependency with Know Who, answering "Who knows this machine well?"
  • Turn veterans' know-how and troubleshooting into assets to maximize output with fewer people

Teachme MySkill

A talent "growth" platform that encourages self-driven skill development. By "gamifying" skill-up, it creates a state where the front line keeps learning on its own—without pressure from headquarters or supervisors—supporting faster onboarding of new and foreign staff and higher retention.

  • Promote skill acquisition with My Skill visualization, My Quests, and EXP / rank-ups
  • View proficiency at a glance with skill maps by organization and store (CSV export)
  • Manage national and external qualifications such as food-hygiene supervisor in one place
  • AI analyzes proficiency and personally suggests the next skill to learn
